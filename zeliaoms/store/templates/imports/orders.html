  • VAT Code (VAT or Non)
  • Discounted Prices (discount amount, optional)
  • Unit Price VAT Incl (price per unit including VAT, e.g., 123)

  • VAT (calculated, e.g., 0)

  • Terms (e.g., Due on Receipt, 7 days on invoice)
  • Status (e.g., Closed)
  • Date Fulfilled (e.g., 2023-06-11, must not be in the future)
  • Payment Amount (e.g., 2,952.00)
  • Payment Ref (optional, e.g., RFT2AKE1VQ)
  • Unpaid Amt? (e.g., 0)
  • Payment Status (e.g., Paid)
  • Date Paid (e.g., 2023-06-29, must not be in the future)
